# Video Recording Package

This folder contains reference frames for recording the UiPath AgentHack demo video.

Use these frames as a storyboard while recording with Loom, YouTube, or Vimeo. The final submission should still be a real screen recording of the running app, not just this screenshot sequence.

## Reference Frames

1. `docs/video-frames/01-overview-uipath.png`
   - Open on the UiPath AgentHack positioning.
   - Show Release Risk, Agent Workflow, Repo Context, Impacted Tests, Human Approval, and Evidence.

2. `docs/video-frames/02-after-risk-scan.png`
   - After clicking `Run Risk Scan`.
   - Narrate how risk and evidence are turned into a governed QA workflow.

3. `docs/video-frames/03-after-generate-tests.png`
   - After clicking `Generate Tests`.
   - Narrate Codex-style generated test work and the future UiPath Test Cloud execution path.

4. `docs/video-frames/04-after-approval.png`
   - After clicking `Approve Release`.
   - Narrate human accountability and UiPath-governed approval.

5. `docs/video-frames/05-public-github-repo.png`
   - Show the public GitHub repository and README.
   - Confirm that setup instructions, submission materials, deck, and license are available.

## Recommended Recording Flow

Follow `docs/DEMO_TELEPROMPTER.md` and `DEMO_SCRIPT.md`.

Keep the video under 5 minutes. Avoid showing private browser tabs, email, credentials, or UiPath tenant details.

## Upload

Upload the final video to YouTube, Vimeo, or Loom. Then paste the video URL into the Devpost project form before finalization.
